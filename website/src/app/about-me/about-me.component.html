About Me

Swisscom

Since August 2019 I've been working as a software/ web developer for Swisscom AG, Switzerland's leading telecommunication company. My training with them will last until summer 2021.

Living in Zurich

Never have I thought that someday I would settle down in Zurich, but until this day I can't share the thought of regretting it. I've moved from Basel to Zurich in 2016, back then for University. Today I live together with my better half in our apartment and enjoy the time with friends and family.

Growing Up

Most of my years I spent growing up in Arlesheim; a small, but very charming village located south of Basel, Switzerland. There I visited state school until 2011, from where I proceeded with my A-Levels in Basel. After graduating it was time to head "out of town" and find my luck up north in Sheffield, UK.
